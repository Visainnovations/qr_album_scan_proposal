## 1. Executive Summary

**Visainnovations** is pleased to present this proposal for the development of **QR Album Scan**, a comprehensive digital photo album platform designed specifically for photo studios. This platform will enable studios to create digital flip books, generate QR codes for easy sharing, and manage their orders efficiently.

## 2. Project Overview

### 2.1 Problem Statement

Photo studios face challenges in:
- Delivering digital albums to clients efficiently
- Managing multiple orders and bookings
- Providing modern digital viewing experience
- Sharing albums easily with clients

### 2.2 Proposed Solution

A web-based platform that offers:
- **Digital Flip Book Viewer** - Interactive album viewing with page-flip animations
- **QR Code Generation** - Instant sharing via scannable QR codes
- **Studio Dashboard** - Complete order and booking management
- **Admin Panel** - Platform administration and analytics
- **Mobile Responsive** - Works seamlessly on all devices

### 2.3 Key Features

| Module | Features |
|--------|----------|
| **Public Website** | Home, Features, Pricing, Contact pages |
| **Studio Portal** | Dashboard, Orders, Bookings, Settings |
| **Admin Panel** | Studios Management, Orders, Analytics, Settings |
| **Album Viewer** | Flip Book, QR Access, Background Music, Social Sharing |
